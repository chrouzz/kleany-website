1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Ojie shopping LLC ("Company," "we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects information in connection with our use of the Amazon Selling Partner API ("SP-API").

We use the Amazon SP-API solely to access and manage data from our own Amazon Seller account for internal business purposes. We do not offer software or services to third parties and do not access data from other sellers.

2. Information We Access

Through the Amazon SP-API, we may access the following types of information related to our own seller account:

  • Order information
  • Product listing information
  • Inventory data
  • Fulfillment data
  • Financial and settlement reports
  • Performance metrics
  • Limited customer order information as provided by Amazon

We access only the data necessary to operate and manage our Amazon business.

3. How We Use Information

We use accessed information solely for legitimate business purposes, including:

  • Managing inventory and product listings
  • Processing and fulfilling orders
  • Monitoring account performance
  • Financial reconciliation and accounting
  • Business analytics and reporting
  • Improving operational efficiency

We do not sell, rent, trade, or transfer Amazon data to third parties for marketing or advertising purposes.

4. Data Sharing

We do not share Amazon SP-API data with third parties except:

  • With service providers (such as accountants, cloud hosting providers, or software tools) who assist in operating our business and are bound by confidentiality obligations
  • As required by law, regulation, subpoena, or legal process
  • To protect our legal rights or comply with applicable legal requirements

Any third-party service providers are granted access only to the extent necessary to perform their services.

5. Data Retention

We retain Amazon SP-API data only as long as necessary to:

  • Fulfill business and operational needs
  • Comply with tax, accounting, and legal obligations
  • Meet Amazon's data protection requirements

When data is no longer required, it is securely deleted or anonymized where feasible.

6. Data Security

We implement reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards designed to protect data against unauthorized access, loss, misuse, or alteration. These safeguards may include:

  • Secure password management
  • Multi-factor authentication
  • Encrypted data storage where applicable
  • Restricted access to authorized personnel only
  • Secure hosting environments

While no system is completely secure, we take commercially reasonable steps to protect the information we access.

7. Children's Information

Our business does not knowingly collect or process personal information directly from children under the age of 13. We only access customer data as provided by Amazon in connection with completed transactions.

8. Compliance with Laws

We are committed to complying with applicable U.S. privacy laws, including but not limited to:

  • The California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), where applicable
  • Federal Trade Commission (FTC) data protection standards
  • Applicable state data protection and breach notification laws

To the extent required by law, individuals may contact us to exercise applicable privacy rights.

9. Data Subject Requests

If you have questions about data associated with an Amazon order, please contact Amazon directly, as Amazon is the primary data controller of customer information.
